Welcome to DollarPal

DollarPal is a simple and intuitive tool to help you plan and track your finances.

Here are some of the key features:

  • Plan and track income, expenses, and wealth on a monthly, weekly, or custom period basis.
  • Get a clear view of how your wealth grows over time.
  • Access detailed charts for an overview of your plans. If you prefer precise numbers, a table format is also available.
  • Use free calculation sheets for drafts or specific tracking needs, such as debt, loans, or travel expenses.
  • Customize your preferred date and number format.
